Training

As days pass by, routine being followed by Shen becomes really difficult. He has to wake up early, does not get to eat enough and follows rigiours training.

At the end of each day, Shen was getting stronger and stronger.

For his good performance, Shen was often allowed to meet his family and friends, to whom he would share his experience.

More than a year passes by and Shen was able to compete with the warriors.

All of Shen's dedication and determination makes him the protector of the village.
