Kung Fu Journey

Life of Shen

Through the mist of the morning, the enemy troops came with full force towards the small village. The soldiers sent by the king along with the villagers were not able to hold them for long. Accepting defeat the villagers tried to run away, just then Shen and the kung fu masters come to the rescue. Using their skills in kung fu they defeat the large army and bring peace to the village.

Splash! Shen suddenly wakes up from his sleep, and realizes all was just a dream.

Being the son of a fisherman, Shen gets up and get to work. Shen rows the boat away from the shore to a place where he finds plenty of fish and start fishing.
