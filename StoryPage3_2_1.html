First step

With all courage left in him, Shen decides he would go.

Shen begins his climb.

After a long time Shen gets tired and decides to take a break.

Just then, Shen turns back to take a look.

Shen is awestruck as he sees the village along with the greenery, beautiful mountains surrounding it, and the river flowing into the sea.

Shen gazed for so long as he had never seen such a beautiful view in his life.

After a long time, Shen was satisfied and realized he way only half way through. If he did not make it in time, the gates would be closed and events would begin.
